💡 KEY INSIGHTS FROM DOCUMENTATION
═════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

PROBLEM SOLVED:
  • Default Spark (FIFO) blocks short jobs behind long jobs
  • Pure SJF causes starvation (long jobs wait forever)
  • Fixed resource tiers don't adapt to workload changes

PASTA SOLUTION:
  • Predictive: ML predicts job duration
  • Adaptive: Sorts by duration + wait time
  • Fair: Aging guarantees no starvation
  • Smart: Dynamic tiers adapt to workload

EMPIRICAL RESULTS:
  • 27% faster than FIFO (171s vs 218s)
  • 61% faster for small jobs (2.1s vs 5.4s)
  • 46% faster for medium jobs (9.8s vs 18.2s)
  • Model accuracy: 88% (improves to 91% with feedback)
